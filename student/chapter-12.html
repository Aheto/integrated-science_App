üìò Domestic Animals in Ghana

Domestic animals are animals raised by humans for food, work, or companionship. In Ghana, they are vital for nutrition, income, and cultural practices.

üîç Observing Physical Characteristics

When examining animals, note:

  • Size: Small (goat) vs. large (cattle)
  • Colour: White, black, brown, spotted
  • Shape: Horns, ears, tail length
  • Feeding habits: Grass-eaters (herbivores), grain-eaters (omnivores)
