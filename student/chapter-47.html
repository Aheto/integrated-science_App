‚Ä¢ Kinetic friction: slows moving objects (e.g., sliding book)
‚Ä¢ Rolling friction: much weaker‚Äîwhy wheels work!

When Is Friction Helpful?

  • Walking (shoes grip the ground)
  • Braking a bicycle (rubber pads grip the wheel)
  • Holding a pen (friction keeps it from slipping)

When Is Friction Harmful?

  • Engine parts wearing out
  • Wasted energy as heat in machines
  • Hard to push heavy furniture
