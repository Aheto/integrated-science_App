üìò What Makes a Plant Cell Unique?

Like animal cells, plant cells have a nucleus, cytoplasm, and cell membrane. But they also have **three special features** that help them survive as green, stationary organisms.

üå± Why Plant Cells Matter

  • The cell wall allows trees to grow tall without collapsing.
  • Understanding plant cells helps improve farming and fight crop diseases.
