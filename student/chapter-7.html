üíß Five Key Roles of the Water Cycle

  1. Provides clean water: Rain refills rivers, lakes, and wells used for drinking and farming.
  2. Regulates weather patterns: Evaporation and condensation drive wind, rain, and temperature balance.
  3. Recharges groundwater: Rain soaks into the soil, refilling underground water tables (e.g., boreholes in Ghana).
  4. Transports nutrients: Water carries minerals from soil to plants and oceans, supporting food chains.
  5. Releases energy: When water vapour condenses into clouds, it releases heat‚Äîwarming the atmosphere and influencing climate.
